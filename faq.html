Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is this workshop about?

This 2-day in-person workshop will equip professionals with practical knowledge and tools to perform Life Cycle Assessments (LCA) in alignment with global sustainability standards. Through interactive sessions, real-life case studies, and hands-on exercises, participants will learn to assess environmental impacts across the product lifecycle from raw material extraction to end-of-life disposal.

2. What practical outcomes can I expect from attending this workshop?

  • Understand how to structure and conduct a complete LCA project
  • Gain hands-on experience with popular LCA software and databases
  • Learn how to interpret and communicate LCA results for decision-making and regulatory needs
  • Be able to identify product hotspots and propose design improvements for sustainability
  • Leave with templates, checklists, and a toolkit you can use in your organization immediately

3. Will I become job-ready or project-ready after this workshop?

Yes, this workshop is designed to give you project-ready skills. While full proficiency in LCA comes with practice, this training will enable you to:

  • Confidently participate in or lead LCA initiatives
  • Contribute to ESG or EPR compliance tasks
  • Support sustainability certifications like EPDs or carbon footprint reporting

It’s particularly valuable if you're working in R&D, product design, supply chain, or ESG roles.

4. Is there a post-workshop support system or community?

  • Access to a private LinkedIn or WhatsApp group with fellow participants and trainers
  • Regular updates and resources to keep you engaged
  • Priority invitations to future webinars, advanced workshops, or certification tracks
  • Email-based support for LCA-related queries for up to 30 days after the event

5. How do I apply what I’ve learned to my organization’s needs?

  • Create an action plan tailored to your company or sector
  • Discuss sector-specific use cases with facilitators
  • Receive guidance on building a case for internal LCA adoption (tools, ROI, timelines)

We also offer post-workshop consulting support on request.

6. What topics will be covered over the 2 days?

  • Introduction to LCA and ISO 14040/44 standards
  • Goal & scope definition
  • Inventory analysis (LCI)
  • Impact assessment (LCIA)
  • Software tools overview (OpenLCA, Ecoinvent, etc.)
  • Hands-on LCA case study using a real product
  • Gain insights into sustainability decision-making using LCA
  • LCA in regulatory and ESG frameworks

7. Who are the facilitators?

The sessions will be led by LCA practitioners and sustainability consultants with real-world experience in deploying LCA across manufacturing value chains.
