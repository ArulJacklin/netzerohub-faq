Frequently Asked Questions — Life Cycle Assessment Bootcamp

This 2-day in-person workshop will equip professionals with practical knowledge and tools to perform Life Cycle Assessments (LCA) in alignment with global sustainability standards. Through interactive sessions, real-life case studies, and hands-on exercises, participants will learn to assess environmental impacts across the product lifecycle from raw material extraction to end-of-life disposal.

  • Understand how to structure and conduct a complete LCA project
  • Gain hands-on experience with popular LCA software and databases
  • Learn how to interpret and communicate LCA results for decision-making and regulatory needs
  • Be able to identify product hotspots and propose design improvements for sustainability
  • Leave with templates, checklists, and a toolkit to use immediately
